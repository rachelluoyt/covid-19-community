A SARS-CoV-2 B.1.1.345 variant carrying the E484K mutation was detected in four
patients with no apparent epidemiological association from a hospital network in
upstate New York. Subsequent analysis identified an additional eleven B.1.1.345
variants from this region between December 2020 and February 2021..
